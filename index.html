KA ARTISTICS!

Meet Aivan Kurt S. Ordoñez, professionally known as Kurt Animate. He is a young Filipino Businessman, Animator, and Multimedia Artist who builds the digital worlds others only dream of.

Based in the Philippines, Kurt blends traditional storytelling with modern digital flair, focusing on architecting digital spaces through 3D design and motion graphics.

He is currently a Grade 11 ICT student at the Southern Philippines Institute of Science and Technology, working to bridge the gap between technical code and creative narratives.

When he isn't in "the lab," Kurt is an active volleyball player, often finding inspiration in the physics and agility of the sport to improve his animation timing.

This portfolio is a gateway to his latest projects, including qualitative research on smartwatch user experiences and professional multimedia services for his brand, Kurt Animate Studios.
