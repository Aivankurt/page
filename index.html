WHATSUP MGA KA ARTISTICS!

Meet Aivan Kurt—better known in the digital world as Kurt Animate. A young Filipino Businessman and Pinoy Animator who doesn't just follow trends; he builds the world they live in.

Currently navigating the technical landscape as a Grade 11 ICT student at SPIST, Kurt's mission is simple: to bridge the gap between complex code and captivating storytelling.

As an active Vlogger and Multimedia Artist, his work is a high-speed collision of color and motion. Through his lens, every project becomes a digital narrative.

But it's not all business and keyframes. Kurt is a goofy, high-energy individual who values authenticity over everything else, whether he's on the volleyball court or architecting in Minecraft.

This portfolio is your gateway into the mind of a creator who is just getting started. Welcome to the movement.
